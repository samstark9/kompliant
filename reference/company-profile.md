## Identity

- Carrier: Ambervale Life Insurance Company ("Ambervale Life")
- Founded: 1979
- Field model: commission-only, 1099 independent contractor sales force. There are no employees in the field organization. Every agent, from Associate Agent to Principal Agent, holds an independent contractor agreement with the home office.
- Sales model: hybrid. Appointments are set by phone and run over video or in person, by member preference.
- Lead model: affinity partnerships with professional and alumni associations. A mailing goes to the membership; a no-cost member item serves as the lead magnet. Member responses become sales leads, which are made available to agencies for distribution.
- Products in recruiting context: individual life insurance and supplemental health. Recruiting copy references products only at the category level.
